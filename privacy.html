Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Who We Are

Lorium is an independent apparel brand based in DFW, Texas. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit loriumarchive.com or sign up for SMS notifications.

Information We Collect

When you sign up for SMS notifications, we collect your name and phone number. We do not collect payment information, browsing history, or any additional personal data through our signup forms.

How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is used solely to send you Lorium product updates, drop notifications, and brand communications. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

SMS Communications

By subscribing, you consent to receive recurring automated text messages from Lorium. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance.

Data Storage

Your information is stored securely through Klaviyo, our marketing platform. You can review Klaviyo's privacy policy at klaviyo.com/privacy.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email below or replying STOP to any SMS message.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at contact@loriumarchive.com.
