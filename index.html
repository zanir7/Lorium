The Brand

Architectural streetwear built on a foundation that outlasts the moment.

Lorium is an independent architectural streetwear label out of DFW, Texas, founded on a single principle: foundational luxury. Each garment is engineered before it is styled — its quality embedded in the construction rather than announced through marketing. The result is wear that becomes more legible with time, not less.

The QZ-001 is the first record. A heavyweight quarter zip cut in 400–420 GSM French Terry, finished with a structured stand-up collar and a triangular zinc-alloy zipper pull cast from a pattern Lorium drew from scratch — not sourced, not adapted. Raised collegiate embroidery sits flush at the chest. Nothing is badged.

Foundational luxury, in this house, is not a status signal and not a logo on hardware. It is the technology held inside the garment — the weight of the fleece, the angle of the collar, the cast of the metal — discoverable by anyone who looks closely enough to find it.
